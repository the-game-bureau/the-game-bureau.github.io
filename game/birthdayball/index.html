🎂 Birthday Ball

🛠 Equipment

  • A live American Football game on TV
  • $1 bills — all players start with the same amount
  • Two pins: RUN and PASS

🏈 Played on 1st downs only (~30–40 per game)

1. Ante Up

Every player places $1 on either the RUN pin or the PASS pin. The Birthday Person antes too.

2. The Call

The Birthday Person (BP) declares RUN, PASS, or TOUCHDOWN. They may change their mind until the ball is snapped.

3. Payout

  • Success: Play matches call → BP takes all money on that pin. Other pin stays (rolls over).
  • Failure: Play doesn't match → nothing collected. Both pins roll over.
  • Flag: Penalty flag (accepted, declined, or picked up) = dead play. Nothing collected. Both pins roll over. Rotate.

🏆 Touchdown — "Lagniappe"

BP calls "TOUCHDOWN" — if any TD is scored on that play (either team), BP sweeps all money on both pins. If no TD, it's a failure and both pins roll over.

📖 Definitions

  • RUN: No forward pass attempted — handoff, sack, scramble, QB kneel, lateral, botched handoff
  • PASS: Any forward pass attempt — completion, incompletion, interception, spike

🔄 Rotate

Pass the pins (and rollover cash) to the next player for the next 1st down.

🏆 End of Game

  • Eliminated: Run out of bills → out of rotation
  • Resign: Any player may quit immediately before their turn as BP — they keep their cash
  • Winner: Most cash when the game ends
  • Final Pot: Money left on pins at 0:00 → donate to charity
