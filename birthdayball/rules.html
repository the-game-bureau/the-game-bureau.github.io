## 🎂 Birthday Ball **A high-stakes football-watching game inspired by the New Orleans money-pinning tradition.** --- ### 🛠 Equipment * **Live American Football Game** * **Bankroll:** $1 bills or play money. **All players must start with the same agreed-upon amount** (Recommended: **$20–$30** per player). * **The Pins:** Two clips (clothespins or binder clips) labeled **RUN** and **PASS**. ### 🏈 Setup * **The Rotation:** Establish a turn order (clockwise in a room or down the row at a stadium). * **The Birthday Person:** The current player holding the pins. ### 🕹 How to Play **This game is played ONLY on 1st downs.** There are usually 30 to 40 per game for both teams combined. **1. The Pinning** * Before a 1st down snap, the **Birthday Person** presents the pins. * **All players** (including the Birthday Person) must ante $1 by clipping it to **either** the RUN pin or the PASS pin. **2. The Call** * The Birthday Person announces their prediction: **“I’m going RUN”** or **“I’m going PASS.”** * They may change their call until the ball is snapped. **3. The Payout** * **SUCCESS:** If the play matches the call, the Birthday Person takes **ALL money on that pin.** Money on the other pin stays attached (**Rolls Over**). * **FAILURE:** If the play does not match the call, the Birthday Person takes **nothing.** All money on both pins stays attached (**Rolls Over**). **4. The "Flag" Rule** * If a penalty flag is thrown before or during the play (regardless of if it is accepted, declined, or picked up), the turn is **dead**. The Birthday Person takes nothing. Both pins **Roll Over** to the next player, who plays the resulting next 1st down. **5. Rotate** * Pass the pins (and any rollover cash) to the next player in the rotation for the next 1st down. --- ### 📖 Definitions * **RUN:** No forward pass attempted (handoffs, sacks, scrambles, QB kneels, laterals, botched handoffs, etc.). * **PASS:** Any forward pass attempt (completions, incompletions, interceptions, spikes, etc.). --- ### 🏆 Winning, Resigning, & The End * **Elimination:** If a player runs out of bills, they are **done** and out of the rotation. * **Resigning:** A player may quit the game at any time **immediately before their turn** as the Birthday Person starts. They keep their current cash and are removed from the rotation. * **The Champion:** The player with the most cash at the end of the game wins. * **The Final Pot:** Any money remaining on the pins when the game clock hits **0:00** is **donated to charity.** --- ### 🎭 Gameplay Option * **The "Lagniappe" (Touchdown):** Before the snap, the Birthday Person can call **"Touchdown!"** instead of Run or Pass.
