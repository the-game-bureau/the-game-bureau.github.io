Whether you're a newcomer intrigued by the premise or a seasoned investigator seeking deeper understanding, Jack Martin's Dossier serves as your gateway to unlocking the secrets and stories that lie at the heart of Oswald's Diary.

This meticulously curated collection of documents, interviews, and historical records offers a crucial component of your research into Oswald's Diary.

Even if you're not well-versed in Kennedy history, you'll find yourself immersed in the captivating journey of unraveling secrets and uncovering truths.

The dossier will also help set the tone for the game.
