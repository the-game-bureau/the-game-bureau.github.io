Our philosophy

The technology should disappear. The atmosphere should not.

01

Human first

Every game is designed so that the most interesting moments happen between players — not between a player and a screen.

02

Minimum viable technology

We use tech only where it adds clear value. Our best mechanic? A text message.

03

Peculiar by design

We're not trying to beat mainstream game studios. We're making the game you'll be talking about at dinner.

We make games that use just enough technology to open the door, then get out of the room.
— The Game Bureau manifesto

How it works

Easy to enter. Hard to stop thinking about.

The rules are quick. The tone does the heavy lifting. Once the room buys in, the game starts making its own weather.

01

Pick a game

Browse our collection of real-world and text-based games. Each one is designed to fit your group and setting.

02

Gather your people

Most of our games are built for 2–8 players. The fun scales with the group — strangers welcome.

03

Play anywhere

Living room, backyard, dive bar, or city street. Our games are designed to go where you go.

04

Talk about it later

The real victory condition: a story worth telling. Every game is engineered to produce one.
